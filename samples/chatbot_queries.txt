# Sample Chatbot Queries for Testing

## Basic Questions

1. What are our total emissions?
2. Show me our carbon footprint
3. How much CO2 did we emit this month?
4. What is our biggest source of emissions?

## Trend Analysis

5. Why did emissions increase in June?
6. What caused the spike last month?
7. Are our emissions trending up or down?
8. Compare this month to last month
9. Show me year-over-year trends
10. What's our emission trajectory?

## Category Questions

11. Which department has the highest emissions?
12. How much do we emit from business travel?
13. What are our Scope 3 emissions?
14. Show me electricity usage
15. What about our cloud computing emissions?
16. How much fuel do we consume?

## Reduction Strategies

17. Suggest ways to reduce emissions
18. What are some quick wins?
19. How can we lower our carbon footprint?
20. What should we prioritize?
21. Give me actionable recommendations
22. What's the fastest way to reduce emissions?

27. What if all employees work from home 2 days per week?

30. What if we switch to LED lighting?

## Comparative Questions

31. How do we compare to industry average?
32. Which scope has the highest emissions?
33. Is Scope 2 or Scope 3 higher?

35. What's worse: flights or cloud computing?

## Detailed Analysis

36. Break down emissions by category
37. Show me emissions by department
38. What's driving our carbon footprint?
39. Analyze our travel patterns
40. Why is electricity usage so high?
41. What's the root cause of increased emissions?

## Goal Setting

42. Can we reach net zero by 2030?
43. What would it take to reduce emissions by 25%?
44. How do we get to carbon neutral?
45. What's needed for 50% reduction?

## Cost Questions

46. What's the ROI on renewable energy?
47. How much would switching save us?
48. What's the cheapest way to reduce emissions?
49. Compare cost vs impact of recommendations

## Data Questions

51. How many documents have we processed?
52. What types of emissions are we tracking?
53. How accurate are these calculations?

## Implementation Questions

54. How do I implement these recommendations?
55. What are the prerequisites for switching to solar?
56. How long will optimization take?
57. What's the implementation roadmap?

## Compliance & Reporting

58. Are we compliant with GHG Protocol?
59. Generate a carbon report
60. What should we report to stakeholders?
61. Show me our ESG metrics

## Industry-Specific

62. What's typical for our industry?
63. Are we above or below average?

65. How do other companies reduce emissions?

## Actionable Next Steps

66. What should we do first?
67. Give me a 30-60-90 day plan
68. What can we do right now?
69. What's the action plan?
70. Prioritize by quick wins

## Testing Different Tones

71. In simple terms, what's our carbon footprint?
72. Explain like I'm 5: why should we care about emissions?
73. Give me the executive summary
74. Detailed technical analysis of our emissions
75. Brief bullet points of our status

## Edge Cases

76. What if we do nothing?
77. Worst case scenario?
78. Best case scenario?
79. What's the maximum possible reduction?
80. What if regulations change?

## Follow-up Questions

81. Tell me more about that
82. Explain further
83. What else?
84. Any other suggestions?
85. What did you mean by that?

## Complex Multi-Part Questions

86. What are our top 3 emission sources and how can we reduce them?
87. Compare our Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions and suggest the best reduction strategy for each
88. If we implement the top 5 recommendations, what would be our new emission level?
89. Show me trends, identify the problem, and suggest solutions
90. What's our carbon footprint, how does it compare, and what should we do?

## Scenario Combinations

91. What if we reduce travel by 30% AND switch to renewable energy?
92. Combine virtual meetings with cloud optimization - what's the total impact?
93. If we do all quick wins simultaneously, what happens?

## Proactive Questions

94. Alert me if emissions increase by 10%
95. What should we watch out for?
96. Any red flags in our data?
97. What are we missing?

## Benchmarking

98. How do we rank among similar companies?
99. What's the industry standard?
100. Are we leaders or laggards in sustainability?

## Usage Tips

- Start with simple questions (1-10) to warm up
- Test trend analysis (11-20) after uploading sample data
- Try scenario questions (23-30) on the Scenarios page
- Use complex questions (86-90) to test the AI's reasoning
- Follow up questions (81-85) test conversation context
- Edge cases (76-80) test the system's boundaries

## Expected Behavior

The chatbot should:
✓ Answer naturally in conversational tone
✓ Provide specific numbers when available
✓ Offer insights beyond just data
✓ Suggest actionable next steps
✓ Maintain context across follow-ups
✓ Admit when it doesn't have enough data
✓ Redirect to appropriate features when needed
